About Me

My name is Mohamad Nur Hidayat Firdaus bin Majedy. I have worked in the IT industry for more than 2 years and have been self-learning ever since.

Since high school I have been fascinated by the rapid growth of IT technology around me, especially since the first introduction of internet to the world. From there I can see how the internet took its shape and became what we see today.

After high school, I enrolled into college which specializes in computer studies where I learned the basics of computer and most importantly programming. During this time is where I learned to create my first website. It was a bit complicated then compared to today’s standard where nowadays there are numerous ready to use libraries and frameworks which significantly increase the efficiency of web development.

Graduated high school, I continued my studies overseas and took an IT course in Web Media Tech. Here I was exposed with multimedia tools which I never experienced before which comprise of animation and video & audio. Also, during this time I was introduced on how to create a dynamic website using both back-end and front-end technology which helps expanding the prospect on what kind of system we can add into the website.

After I have finished my degree I applied for training for 6 months, which the training includes management and software development. Then, I joined a startup company which focus on web app development and after more than 10 months, I was offered to join an internship by BSDC in a one-year contract. During this time, me and my team were given training for Microsoft Certification and tasked to create POC system to the government bodies.

Since my contract ended, I currently learning new technology on my own to broadened my prospect which not only comprising in web development, but in game and mobile development as well.
